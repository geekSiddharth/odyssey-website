Description
The cultural fest of IIIT Delhi, Odyssey '17, in collaboration with Mr. Bobby Joshi, one of India's finest travel photographers is organising a photography workshop.

The 5-hour workshop will be divided into two parts: Photography techniques(Basics, Intermediate) and Post processing. The first part will include the following agenda:
  • Rule of Thirds and tenths
  • Light controlling
  • Image construction
  • Light control
  • The triangle
  • Travel photo tour planning, and many more

The post processing part will consist of techniques like Light enhancement, Depth creation, Exposure blend, Dark and Rich mood and 16-bit photography. There a lot more things lined up in this workshop. It will be highly interactive and will have in-class sessions.
